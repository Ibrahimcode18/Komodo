Javan Rhinoceros: A Species on the Brink

Scientific Name: Rhinoceros sondaicus

📍 Habitat & Range

Javan rhinos are found **only in Ujung Kulon National Park**, Java, Indonesia. They prefer **dense tropical forests with plenty of mud and water sources**.

🦏 Physical Characteristics

Javan rhinos are **smaller than other rhino species**, with a **single horn** and tough, armor-like skin.

🍖 Diet & Behavior

Javan rhinos are **herbivores**, eating **leaves, shoots, and fruit**. They are shy and prefer to **stay hidden in thick vegetation**.

⚠️ Threats & Conservation

With **fewer than 80 individuals left**, Javan rhinos are critically endangered due to **habitat destruction and poaching**.
