Sumatran Tiger: The Last of Its Kind

Scientific Name: Panthera tigris sumatrae

📍 Habitat & Range

Native to Sumatra, Indonesia, Sumatran tigers live in dense rainforests, grasslands, and swampy areas.

🐯 Physical Characteristics

The smallest of all tiger subspecies, Sumatran tigers have thicker fur and darker stripes, which provide excellent camouflage in dense vegetation.

🍖 Diet & Hunting

Sumatran tigers are solitary hunters, preying on deer, wild boar, and monkeys. They are incredibly strong, able to drag prey much heavier than themselves.

⚠️ Threats & Conservation

Fewer than 400 Sumatran tigers remain, with deforestation and poaching being the biggest threats.
