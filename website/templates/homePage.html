Why Komodo Matters

Education & Awareness

Developing comprehensive conservation curricula for schools and universities across Indonesia to foster the next generation of environmental stewards.

  • Interactive learning modules
  • Teacher training programs
  • Student research grants

Community Empowerment

Engaging local communities in sustainable conservation efforts that provide economic alternatives to habitat destruction.

  • Wildlife monitoring training
  • Eco-tourism initiatives
  • Sustainable farming workshops

Scientific Research

Supporting cutting-edge research on endangered species and their ecosystems to inform conservation strategies.

  • Habitat protection studies
  • Population tracking
  • Conservation technology
Sumatran Tiger

Critically endangered with fewer than 100 individuals remaining, Threats include habitat loss, poaching, and human–wildlife conflict.

Javan Rhinoceros

One of the world's rarest large mammals, with an extremely limited population, primarily in Ujung Kulon National Park. Faces severe risks from habitat degradation and disease.

Komodo Dragon

The world’s largest lizard, found only on a few Indonesian islands. Despite their fearsome reputation, habitat loss and declining prey populations threaten their survival.

Bawean Deer

A shy and elusive deer found only on Bawean Island, threatened by habitat destruction.

Orangutans

Both Sumatran and Bornean orangutans are critically endangered due to deforestation and the palm oil industry.

Anoa

Indonesia’s "dwarf buffalo," found in Sulawesi’s forests, is facing severe population decline

Tarsius

A small primate with large eyes, found in limited regions. Vulnerable to deforestation and habitat disturbance, making them highly susceptible to environmental changes

Celebes Crested Macaque

An endangered primate native to Sulawesi. Threatened by habitat loss, hunting, and human conflict, with a declining population due to these.

Sumatran Elephant

A subspecies of the Asian elephant, losing its habitat to deforestation and human-wildlife conflict
