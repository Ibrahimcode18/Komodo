About Us

Welcome to Komodo Hub – your digital gateway to animal conservation in Indonesia. Developed by Yayasan Komodo, our platform is dedicated to protecting Indonesia’s most endangered endemic species, including the Sumatran Tiger, Javan Rhinoceros, Bali Myna, Javan Eagle, Tarsius, and Celebes Crested Macaques. We believe that conservation is a shared responsibility, and Komodo Hub empowers communities, schools, and individuals to play an active role in preserving our natural heritage.

At Komodo Hub, we offer a rich knowledge base on endangered species along with interactive tools that enable users to contribute through content sharing, event participation, and reporting sightings. Whether you are a teacher integrating conservation into your curriculum, a student eager to engage in hands-on learning, or a community member passionate about wildlife protection, our platform provides a collaborative space to learn, connect, and act.

Our mission is to harness innovative technology to transform digital engagement into tangible conservation outcomes, ensuring that every contribution makes a lasting impact. Join us as we work together to secure a sustainable future for Indonesia’s wildlife and inspire positive change for generations to come
