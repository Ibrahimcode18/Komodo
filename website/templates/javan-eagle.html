Javan Eagle: The Pride of Indonesia

Scientific Name: Nisaetus bartelsi

📍 Habitat & Range

The Javan Eagle is **found only in Java, Indonesia**, living in **mountain forests and tropical rainforests**.

🦅 Physical Characteristics

Javan Eagles are **medium-sized raptors with a distinct crest** and **brown plumage**.

🍖 Diet & Hunting

Javan Eagles hunt **small mammals, reptiles, and birds**, using their **powerful talons**.

⚠️ Threats & Conservation

Deforestation and **illegal wildlife trade** have put the Javan Eagle at risk.
